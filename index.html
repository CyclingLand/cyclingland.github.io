Why design is important

Design isn't just how it looks, It's a lot more than that. For example: something can look realy nice but it can distract you from the things that are more important. That way users miss a lot of critical things. Even though it seems so easy, sometimes it's the biggest issue some people just don't consider. If you have a bad UI people might be confused and just leave. And that isn't what you want right. Design is more than placing some things somewhere in the hopes it looks decent. There has to go a lot of tought in. Personally, I really like the design of IOS. It's all the same kind of UI, wich may sound boring but it's the key for users to know where to go.
