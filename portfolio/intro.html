Intros & Outros

Your intro and outro can give your channel that nice look. But it can also have the opposite effect. If you have an intro longer than 10 secondes, it can become boring and people will just skip it or even worse: click away from the video. That's why I mostly keep my intro's between 6 and 8 seconds. The outrois very important, because it can lead people to action. For example, if you have a webshop you can add it into the outro using YouTube's Endscreens. You can also put in links to other videos or to subscribe to your channel.
