Designs for Vlogbattle Breda 2017

The Vlogbattle is a contest were beginning creators can show there skills and compete against other creators. There are workshops and Meet & Greets with bigger YouTubers. The main intention is that the starting creators can develop their creativities and passion.

Vlogbattle is created by creators, for creators.

Instagram posts

These images are designed for the Instagram page of the vlogbattle, their main form of comunication with the participants. The Line-up posts contained the name and picture of a creator that would come to the event. There were also information posts about the diffrent themes and the venue.
