Banners & Avatars

There a lot of ways you can call this, but let's just keep it on branding. Your branding is what people will recognize. It will let you stand out. Your branding is also important to make your channel unique. Your Banner is the large image on your YouTube channel. This is only visible if you view the whole channel. So keep in mind that not everyone will see it. I see in my statistics that on average 30% of my view traffic comes from the channel page. So those people will also see my banner. On the banner you can put stuff like upload dates, upcoming events, a short description of your content. And with short I mean 2 or 3 words. So yeah, keep it simple. Your banner shouldn't be loaded with text, because as we said earlier that will lead to confusion. On this website you can find some guidelines and great examples.
